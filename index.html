The Middlemist Family Cookbook — Guide
Welcome to the family cookbook
This cookbook lives on your device as a single file. Below is a guide to everything it can do.
Browsing & finding recipes
🔍
Search
Type anything in the sidebar search bar — recipe names, ingredients, or an attribution like "Grandma Mae". Results appear instantly.
🏷️
Filter & Sort
Open any category and use the tag pills to filter (e.g. "Holiday", "No-Cook") and the sort menu to order A→Z or by recently added.
✦
Surprise me
Tap "Surprise me" in the sidebar for a random recipe — great for "what should I make tonight?"
★
Favourites
Tap the heart icon on any recipe to save it. Find all your favourites via the sidebar link.
Cooking a recipe
⚖️
Scale ingredients
Use the scale bar on any recipe to adjust serving size — ½×, 1×, 2×, 3× or custom. Quantities update automatically with fraction display.
👨‍🍳
Cooking mode
Tap the star icon to enter full-screen cooking mode. Steps advance one at a time, ingredients are checkable, and your screen stays awake.
🖨️
Print
Tap the printer icon for a clean print view — just the recipe, no sidebars or buttons.
📤
Share
Tap the share icon to send a recipe via text, email, or copy it to your clipboard.
Shopping list
On any recipe, tap "Add to shopping list" to open a picker showing all ingredients — pre-checked by default. Select what you need and tap "Add selected". Items from multiple recipes build up in the list together, grouped by recipe. Tap any item to check it off as you shop. Tap Share to send the list, or Copy text to paste it into a message. Access the list anytime from the sidebar.
Family stories & cooked log
📖
Family Stories
Every recipe has a Family Stories section at the bottom. Write memories, variations, who taught you the recipe — these notes are saved on your device and print with the recipe.
⏱
Cooked log
Tap "Made this today" to log the date you cooked a recipe. The log builds over time so you can see when you last made something.
Adding & editing recipes
＋
Add a recipe
Tap "Add new recipe" in the sidebar to add your own recipe. Fill in the form and tap Save — it's stored on your device immediately.
✉️
Submit to editor
Want a recipe added for everyone? Fill out the form and tap "Submit to cookbook editor". This opens your mail app with the recipe pre-written — just hit send.
📸
Recipe photos
Add a photo to any recipe using the photo zone on the recipe page, or through the add/edit form.
✏️
Edit or delete
Open any recipe and use the edit (pencil) or delete (trash) icons in the top bar. Deletion requires the cookbook password.
A note on saving: Recipes you add, notes, favourites, and your shopping list are all saved in your browser's local storage. They stay on this device and browser. To share your additions with the whole family, use "Submit to cookbook editor" and the cookbook editor will add them to the main file.
Submit recipe to editor

This will open your email app with the recipe pre-filled and ready to send to the cookbook editor. Once reviewed it will be added to the main cookbook for everyone.

Before you submit:
• Make sure you've filled in the title and ingredients
• Add an attribution so we know where the recipe came from
• You can attach a photo of the recipe in your email app
