The Middlemist Family Cookbook — Guide
Welcome to the family cookbook
A living family cookbook — recipes, stories, and memories all in one place, shared across every device in real time.
Browsing & finding recipes
Search
Type anything in the sidebar search bar — recipe names, ingredients, or an attribution like "Grandma Mae". Results appear instantly.
Filter & Sort
Open any category and use the tag pills to filter by occasion or ingredient, and sort A→Z or by recently added.
Surprise me
Tap "Surprise me" in the sidebar for a random recipe — great for "what should I make tonight?"
Favourites
Tap the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your personal favourites. Sign in to keep them synced across devices. Only you can see your favourites.
Cooking a recipe
Scale ingredients
Use the scale bar on any recipe to adjust serving size — ½×, 1×, 2×, or 3×. All quantities update automatically.
Cooking mode
Tap the pot icon to enter full-screen cooking mode. Steps advance one at a time, ingredients are checkable, and your screen stays awake.
Print
Tap the printer icon for a clean print view — just the recipe, no navigation or buttons.
Share
Tap the share icon to send a recipe via iMessage, email, or copy the link to your clipboard.
Shopping list
Add to list
On any recipe, tap "Add to shopping list" to open a picker. All ingredients are pre-checked — select what you need and tap "Add selected".
Share your list
In the shopping list, tap "Copy text" to paste into a message, or "Share" to send via your phone's share sheet. Your list is private to your device.
Family stories & cooked log
Family Stories
Every recipe has a Family Stories section. Write memories, variations, who taught you the recipe. Sign in to save your stories for everyone — they sync in real time.
Cooked log
Tap "Made this today" to log when you cooked a recipe. The log is shared — the whole family can see who cooked what and when.
Adding & editing recipes
Sign in
Tap the Sign in button in the top-right corner of any page. Sign in with your Google account — one tap, no new password needed. Once signed in you can add and edit recipes, write family stories, and log cooking. Your favourites sync across all your devices automatically.
Add a recipe
Tap "Add new recipe" in the sidebar. Fill in the form and tap Save — the recipe is added to the cookbook for everyone immediately.
Edit or delete
Open any recipe and use the pencil (edit) or trash (delete) icons in the topbar. Only the admin can delete recipes — you will be asked to confirm before anything is removed.
Original family recipes
Recipes marked with a ⚜ Original Family Recipe badge are dishes that originated with or were long held by the Middlemist family. This flag can only be set or removed by the cookbook admin.
How saving works: Recipes, family stories, and the cooked log sync in real time via Firebase — sign in once and everything is shared with the family. Your shopping list is private to your device. Your favourites are private to your account.
